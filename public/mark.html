Day in the Life of Asset Management Sales

Relationship Manager (RM) receives alert on his iPhone following a newsfeed off a keyword interest (defined contribution)

RM shares article with Community and hashtags with #[Defined Contribution]

Sales Team Coordinator receives email notification and begins further dialog in Community page

Sales Team Coordinator comments and assigns tasks via a collaboration platform list to the appropriate team

Marketing Support Liaison completes analysis and works with Marketing team to assemble collateral package for Salesperson

Salesperson makes sales call to key prospective accounts in territory using the assembled and relevant marketing materials

Salesperson provides feedback into the Community about the sales strategy, materials used, and tactics that worked well
